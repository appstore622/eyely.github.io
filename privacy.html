How Finnote collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Finnote ("Finnote", "we", "the app") handles information when you use our personal finance application on iPhone and iPad. It matches the in-app Privacy Policy.

Summary

  • Your financial data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • No account registration or cloud sync is required.
  • We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • Camera, microphone, and speech recognition are used only when you choose receipt scan or voice entry.
  • AI parsing (Apple Intelligence where available) and receipt OCR (Vision) run on-device.
  • Voice entry uses Apple’s on-device speech recognition when supported by the device.

Information We Process

The app stores the following on your device:

  • Transactions (amount, category, date, notes, merchant)
  • Accounts and balances
  • Budgets, bill reminders, savings goals, and related planner data
  • App settings (language, currency, appearance)
  • Receipt images you choose to attach (stored locally)

We do not operate user accounts or cloud storage for app data, so this information is not uploaded to our servers.

Device Permissions

  • Camera / Photo Library — only when you scan or import a receipt.
  • Microphone — only when you use voice entry.
  • Speech Recognition — only when you use voice entry; processed with Apple’s on-device Speech APIs when available.
  • Notifications — only if you enable bill reminders; scheduled locally on your device.

You can revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Widget Data

The optional Home Screen widget reads a summary snapshot (total balance, today’s income/expense) via Apple’s App Group (group.com.finnote.app). This data stays on your device and is shared only between the main app and its widget extension.

Data Export and Deletion

  • Export — you may export data (CSV / JSON) from Settings.
  • Delete — uninstalling the app removes locally stored data. Individual records can also be deleted in the app.

Children’s Privacy

Finnote is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
