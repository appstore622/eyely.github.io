FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. Finnote works fully offline without registration or login.

Is my data synced to the cloud?

No. All financial data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad. Mac is not available for this release.

How does AI entry work?

Type or speak naturally (e.g. "Starbucks 5.50"). On supported devices with Apple Intelligence, parsing runs on-device. Other devices use a built-in rule engine.

How do I scan receipts?

Open the Receipt tab or use the import shortcut. Grant camera or photo library permission when prompted. OCR runs on-device via Apple's Vision framework.

How do I export or back up my data?

Go to Settings → Export CSV for spreadsheets, or use Export JSON Backup / Import JSON Backup for a full restore. Share files via the system share sheet.

What else is included?

Multi-account tracking, category budgets with subcategories, savings goals, loans & receivables, credit-card installments, shopping lists, spending calendar, cashflow forecast, expense split, bill reminders, and debt planner — all included with the download.

How do I add the home screen widget?

Long-press the home screen → Add Widget → search "Finnote" → choose "Today Summary". Available on iPhone and iPad.

Is there a subscription?

No. Finnote is a one-time paid download with all features included. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Bill reminders are not appearing

Enable notifications for Finnote in iOS Settings, and turn on Bill Reminders in app Settings.

Where is the privacy policy?

Please see our Privacy Policy.
