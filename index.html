Overview

About the app

Finnote (App Store: Finnote - Budget Tracker) is a personal finance app for tracking expenses, income, budgets, and bills. All financial data is stored locally on your device — no account registration or cloud sync is required.

Download once and unlock every feature. There are no subscriptions and no in-app purchases.

Capabilities

What you can do

Offline by design

Your data stays on your device. No account, no forced cloud sync.

AI smart entry

Type or speak naturally — e.g. “Starbucks 5.50”. Parsed on-device where supported.

Receipt scanning

Snap a receipt. On-device OCR extracts amount and merchant.

Budgets & bills

Category budgets, custom subcategories, bill reminders, and debt planner.

Goals & loans

Savings goals, loans & receivables, credit-card installments, and shopping lists.

Insights & backup

Spending calendar, cashflow forecast, expense split, CSV/JSON backup, and Today widget.
